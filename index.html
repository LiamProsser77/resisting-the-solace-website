About Us

The Story

Resisting the Solace is the creative collaboration of Anniken and Barry Prosser. Born from a shared vision of sonic exploration, the project serves as a vessel for atmospheric soundscapes, cinematic intensity, and raw emotional resonance.

The music navigates the delicate balance between shadows and light, blending haunting melodies with powerful production. Each composition is designed to be an immersive experience, inviting the listener into a world where sound tells the story that words often cannot reach.

Operating as a self-contained creative unit, Anniken's evocative vocal delivery meets Barry's intricate guitar work and production. Together, they continue to push the boundaries of their sound, evolving with every transmission.
